About Me

I'm a high school senior passionate about the intersection of finance, entrepreneurship, and creating meaningful impact. From co-founding youth entrepreneurship programs that have reached 150+ students to conducting academic research on climate-driven financial risks, I thrive on solving complex problems and building solutions that make a difference.

Whether I'm analyzing financial markets, leading student initiatives, or competing on the football field, I bring the same drive: curiosity, strategic thinking, and a commitment to excellence. I'm excited to bring this mindset to college and continue pushing boundaries in business and finance.
